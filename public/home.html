Covid Memories

Covid Memories is a curated collection of personal narratives and reflections from the early days of the pandemic. Our Covid Memories Manager tool allows users to manage, add, and remove audio files with playback functionality, without the need of Alexa or other smart speaker devices. This project connects to Google Sheets to provide back-end management of content for those with little to no programming experience. This Covid Memories Demo is currently under construction by undergraduate students from Western Washington University's computer science department.
